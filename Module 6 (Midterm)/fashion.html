Fashion in Destiny

One less thought of, but hugely important, aspect of Destiny is the fashion. Yes, the fashion! Destiny is, after all, a Massive Multiplayer Online (MMO) game which means there is a lot of incentive for players to try and stand out from the crowd. When you join a random fireteam it is always fun to inspect the gear that other players have equipped. You can get a lot of inspiration for your own fashion this way! And next time you play with your private friend group you can try and compete to see who has the best fashion!

Holiday Events to Remember!

  • 05/03/23 Guardian Games
    • Participate to earn Olympics themed armor!
  • 07/19/23 Solstice
    • Participate to earn Solar themed armor!
  • 10/18/23 Festival of the Lost
    • Participate to earn Halloween themed armor!
  • 12/13/23 The Dawning
    • Participate to earn Christmas themed armor!

Discord is another fantastic tool than any Destiny players should become familiar with. It is a great place to makes friends and participate in the gaming community. But above all else... it is the best place to show off your fashion!
