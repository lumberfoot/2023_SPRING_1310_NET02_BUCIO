Destiny The Game

What is Destiny?

Destiny is a popular Massive Multiplayer Online (MMO) video game created by game developer Bungie. The game features a wide range of live service activities for players to login and choose from any day of the week. Regular service and content updates are rolled out every couple of months, culminating with a large DLC expansion at the start of each new year. It is a fun and exciting game to play on your own or with a team of online friends. And if this is your first time playing an online vidoe game, you can always head on over to Bungie Clans and join a clan of your choosing!

  • It supports crossplay across Xbox, PS5, and PC.
  • Play on up to three unique and different classes.
  • Participate in limited time seasonal activities.
  • Purchase cosmetic items from the rotating cash shop.

The Story

Destiny takes place in a futuristic world where the human race has long outlived their golden age and now finds itself at the brink of extinction. But one day an alien lifeform, known as "The Traveler," descended upon planet Earth and gave a select fiew humans the powers of light. With this gift, these chosen few became known as Guardians. They are tasked with the protection of Earth and it is their goal to find a way to prevent the collapse of humanity. However, it is no easy task to protect an entire civilization against extraterrestrial invaders. For this reason, Guardians are equipped with the latest technology and the ability to revive with the help of a Ghost give to them by The Traveler.
